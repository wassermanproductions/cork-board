Top Bar

Project menu
Switch between saved projects.
New
Create a blank project while keeping the others saved.
Presets
Start from feature, TV, short, music video, commercial, or documentary structures.
Find
Search text and filter by character, location, label, status, or deadline. Matching cards stay lit.
History
Save or restore named checkpoints before major restructures.
Save
Save the current project locally. Autosave also runs after every change.
Export
Markdown outline, CSV scene list, Fountain scaffold, or full project JSON.

Board Bar

Board tabs
One board per episode, wall, or draft. Double-click a tab to rename, drag to reorder.
+
Add another board to this project.
Board / Outline / Arcs
Cork board, linear outline, or the character-by-scene arc grid.
Cork
Cycle the surface: cork, paper, or midnight.
Cards
Cycle index card size: S, M, L.
World
Show or hide the cast, places, labels, and insights drawer.

Cards & Columns

Drag a card
Move it anywhere — within a column, across columns, or onto another board tab.
Drag a column
Grab the column header to reorder whole acts or sequences.
Click a card
Open the inspector: synopsis, characters, location, labels, status, due date, checklist, notes.
+ Add card
Quick-add at the bottom of every column. Enter adds and keeps typing.
Double-click header
Rename a column in place.
Collapse
Fold a column to a slim spine to focus elsewhere.
⋯ column menu
Rename, recolor, duplicate the column with its cards, or sort its cards by status, due date, title, or pages.
Due dates
Set a deadline on a card — the flag turns amber when it's close and red when it's overdue. Locked and Cut cards stop alarming.
Drag a cast chip
Drop a character from the drawer onto any card to tag them into the scene.

Drawer

Cast
Characters with color, role, want/need, and arc. Click to edit, drag onto cards to tag.
Places

Labels

Insights
